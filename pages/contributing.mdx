# Contributing

Currently, **Musync!** is not open-source. However, there are various ways to contribute to its development:

- [Cast your vote on Top.gg](https://top.gg/bot/1182507989576458352/vote)
- [Leave a review on Top.gg](https://top.gg/bot/1182507989576458352#reviews)
- [Donate on Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/sinnedpenguin)
- Share your ideas and provide feedback through:
  - [Our Support Server](https://discord.com/invite/yQxpgFdC6n)
  - Email: sinnedpenguin@proton.me

We've invested significant time and effort into building and maintaining **Musync!**. Your support is inspiring to us.

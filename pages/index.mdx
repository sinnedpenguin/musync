  # Musync!
  A <strong>simple</strong> yet <strong>powerful</strong> music bot that brings a seamless music experience to your server. 
</div>

## Features
- **100% Free**
- **High-Quality Music**
- **24/7 Uptime**
- **Simple Controls**
- **Voting System (/skip, /skipto, and /stop)**
- **Autoplay**

## Supported Sources
- **YouTube**
- **Spotify**
- **Deezer**
- **SoundCloud**
